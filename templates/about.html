Welcome! Thank you for visiting my website and helping me out with my dissertation.

The Project

For my dissertation project, I am trying to make an app which can take a photo of any dog and identify its breed using matching learning The upside of this approach is that it is much simpler than an advanced computer vision algorithm, the downside is that it requires vast amounts of training data. For this project, the objective is to be able to identify 269 different dog breeds, and ideally I need at least 300 photos of each breed in order to train the program. 269 * 300 = 80,700 so as you can see there's a lot of data to gather!

That's where you come in!

How to use

  1. Go to the identifier page
  2. You'll be presented with a breed name and 10 photos of that dog. Click/tap all the ones which aren't a clear photo of that dog
  3. Once you're happy with your selections, press the 'Submit' button at the bottom of the page and your response will be saved
  4. Repeat as many times as you like - every little helps!

If you would like to view the project progress, visit the progress page.
